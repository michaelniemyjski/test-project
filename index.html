Within this little excerpt, I will briefly explain:

  1. why digital publishing is important to me
  2. what I think I can get out of digital publishing
  3. what I want from digital publishing

Digital publishing allows my art the possibility to reach a large audience. This is something that I want because I want others to relate to me from an artistic and maybe even a spiritual standpoint.

Publishing is significant to me because of that reason, and it gives me the form of a goal. Knowing that digital publishing opens the door to easily letting hundreds of people see who I am makes that goal more believable.
